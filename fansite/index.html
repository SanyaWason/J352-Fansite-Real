Fun facts about Fleetwood Mac:

  1. Dreams is the only single of theirs to reach #1 on the US Billboard Hot 100 Chart.
  2. Rumors is the 9th best selling album of all time.
  3. They have a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
  4. Yesterday's Gone was the original title of Rumors, derived from the track Don't stop.
  5. They were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1998 by Sheryl Crow.
